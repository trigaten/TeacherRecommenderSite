Teacher Recommender System


Track and control the teacher college recommendation process with 3 easy steps
The Teacher Recommendation System (TRS) is a Google Sheet add-on that allows college counselors to efficiently and fairly connect students with their teacher recommenders for college. In 3 easy steps, counselors can collect data and generate teacher/student pairings.


Benefits for college counselors:

  • Track and control the entire pairing process from within a Google Sheet
  • Ensure a fair process for students and teachers
  • Use Google Forms and automated emails to collect information
  • Quickly view which teachers and students have completed their forms.
  • Easily send reminder emails to teachers and students

Benefits for college counselors:

  • Track and control the entire pairing process from within a Google Sheet
  • Ensure a fair process for students and teachers
  • Use Google Forms and automated emails to collect information
  • Quickly view which teachers and students have completed their forms.
  • Easily send reminder emails to teachers and students
Benefits for students and teachers:

  • Pairings are made between students and teachers who both want to be together
  • Teachers can set limits on their quantity of recommendations
  • Students and teachers complete a Google Form that takes less than 2 minutes

Step 1:
  • Enter the emails of students and teachers into a Google Sheet.
  • Automatically generate a teacher selection form and email it to students.
  • Ask each student to use the form to rank their preferred pairs of teacher recommenders.
Step 2:
  • Generate and send student selection forms to the teachers.
  • Ask the teachers to complete the forms by indicating their confidence in their ability to write a strong letter of recommendation for each student who selected them.
Step 3:
  • After all results have been collected, run the pairing algorithm and automatically send emails to students and teachers with their final results.
