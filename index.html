Teacher Recommender System

Control the teacher recommender process with three easy steps
The Teacher Recommendation System allows college counselors to easily control the process of connecting students with their teacher recommenders for college. Just put the emails of the students and teachers in a Google Sheet and TRS allows you to send them autogenerated forms to collect their preferences. Then, run a simple matching algorithm and automatically send out assignment results.
Step 1:
  • Enter the emails of the students and teachers into a Google Sheet.
  • Click buttons to automatically generate forms and send them to the students.
  • The student form will ask each student to rank pairs of teacher recommenders.
    • Step 2:
  • Click buttons to generate and send teacher forms.
  • These forms will ask the teachers to decide how strongly they feel that they can write a good reccomendation letter for the students that selected them.
    • Step 3:
  • After all results have been collected, run the algorithm, review the results, and automatically send emails to students and teachers with their respective assignments
